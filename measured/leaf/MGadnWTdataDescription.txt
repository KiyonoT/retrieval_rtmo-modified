Concerning files with initial names MG and WT

Recorded in August 2015 by Nastassia Vilfan and Peiqi Yang,
location Campus Klein Altendorf,
in collaboration with FZ Juelich.

Time of measurements: cca 11.30 am

WT stands for Wild type soy bean, with high values of chlorophylls, dark green leaves
MG stands for Marygold variety soybean, with bright green leaves with low chlorophyll values, 
known to have some interesting physiological properties (higher photosynthesis than WT, despite lower chlorophylls)
